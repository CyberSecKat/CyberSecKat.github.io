I'm Kathleen Fell, a cybersecurity professional with a focus on detection
engineering — writing and tuning detections, parsing logs and telemetry,
and building tools that make security teams faster at spotting real threats.
I'm especially interested in Windows/Sysmon telemetry, threat detection
logic, and making security tooling more approachable for teams that don't
have a dedicated detection engineering function yet.

Outside of work I'm building out this site as a place to share projects and
longer-form writing on the things I'm learning.
